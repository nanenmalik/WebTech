LAND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

The Yale School of Art and Yale University acknowledge that indigenous peoples and nations, including:

the Mashantucket Pequot, the Mohegan, the Eastern Pequot, the Schaghticoke, the Golden Hill Paugussett, the Niantic, and the Quinnipiac and other Algonquian speaking peoples have stewarded through generations the lands and waterways of what is now the state of Connecticut. We honor and respect the enduring relationship that exists between these peoples and nations and this land.
